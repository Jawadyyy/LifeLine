Delete your LifeLine account

This page explains how to request deletion of your LifeLine account (the emergency & blood-donation app published on Google Play) and all data associated with it.

How to request deletion

Step 1. Send an email to jawadm.dev@gmail.com with the subject line "LifeLine account deletion request".
Step 2. In the email, include the email address or phone number your LifeLine account is registered with, so we can identify the account.
Step 3. We will confirm and permanently delete your account and data within 30 days.

What is deleted

  • Your account and login credentials
  • Your profile (name, phone number, profile photo)
  • Your Medical ID (blood group, allergies, conditions, and other health information)
  • Your chat messages, photos, and voice notes
  • Your blood-donation posts
  • Your saved emergency contacts and device notification tokens

What may be kept

  • Messages you sent to other users may remain visible in their copy of the conversation, unless you delete them in-app first.
  • Anonymous crash and analytics records that are not linked to your identity.

No additional retention period applies beyond the 30-day processing window.

Deleting some data without deleting your account

Inside the app you can, at any time: delete individual chat messages, delete your blood-donation posts, remove emergency contacts, and edit or clear Medical ID fields.
