LifeLine — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 10 July 2026

LifeLine ("the app", "we") is an emergency assistance and blood-donation coordination app. This policy explains what information the app collects, why, where it is stored, and your choices. LifeLine is a free app with no ads and no sale of personal data.

Information we collect

DataWhy it is collectedWhere it is stored
Account details — name, email address, phone number, profile photo Creating your account, letting other users identify you as a contact or donor Google Firebase (Authentication & Firestore); profile photos on Supabase Storage
Health information — blood group, allergies, medical conditions, height, weight, BMI, emergency note Your Medical ID card, shown to your emergency contacts and used for blood-donor matching. Providing this is optional. Google Firebase (Firestore)
Location — precise device location Only when you trigger an SOS alert, share live location, or use the donation map. Location is not tracked in the background outside an active live-share you start. Google Firebase (Firestore); addresses resolved via Geoapify
Contacts — names and phone numbers from your address book Only when you choose to add an emergency contact, to find which of your contacts use LifeLine. Your address book is not copied to our servers; only contacts you explicitly add are saved. Google Firebase (Firestore)
Messages — chat text, photos, voice notes The in-app chat between you and your contacts Text in Google Firebase (Firestore); photos and voice notes on Supabase Storage
Voice calls In-app voice calls between contacts. Call audio is transmitted in real time via Agora and is not recorded or stored. Call metadata (who called whom, when, duration) is stored for your chat history. Metadata in Google Firebase (Firestore); audio transit via Agora
AI assistant conversations Questions you type to the in-app medical assistant are sent to Google's Gemini API to generate a reply Processed by Google; assistant chat history stored on your device
Device push token, crash reports, basic analytics Delivering notifications (SOS alerts, messages, incoming calls), fixing crashes, understanding basic usage Google Firebase (Cloud Messaging, Crashlytics, Analytics)

How your information is shared

  • With people you choose: your name, phone number, profile photo, Medical ID, location (during SOS/live share), messages and calls are shared with the emergency contacts and chat partners you add or message.
  • With blood-donation users: if you create a donation post, its details (name, blood group, location, contact) are visible to other users so they can respond.
  • With service providers that run the app's infrastructure: Google Firebase, Supabase, Agora, Geoapify, Google Gemini, and Vercel (notification relay). They process data on our behalf and under their own security terms.
  • We do not sell personal data, and we do not share it with advertisers.

Permissions the app requests

  • Location — SOS alerts, live location sharing, donation map
  • Contacts — adding emergency contacts
  • Microphone — voice notes and voice calls
  • Camera / Photos — profile picture and chat photos
  • Notifications — SOS alerts, messages, incoming calls

Each permission is requested only when the related feature is first used, and the app remains usable if you decline (the related feature is disabled).

Data retention and deletion

Your data is kept while your account exists. To delete your account and all associated data (profile, Medical ID, messages, media, donation posts), contact us at the email below and we will remove it within 30 days. You can also delete individual messages and donation posts inside the app at any time.

Security

All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (TLS). Access to stored data is restricted by server-side security rules so users can only read data intended for them (e.g. only a chat's two participants can read its messages).

Children

LifeLine is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us and it will be removed.
